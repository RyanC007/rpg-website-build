2. Strategy/SEO/Cleanup — We build your SEO strategy, identify keywords, fix gaps, and create your content roadmap.
3. Knowledge Base — Your Business Backpack. The foundation that makes everything work. Take it anywhere.
4. Launch Content — We publish consistent, optimized content every week to keep you visible and growing.

## Pain Points Section
- "Why your business is not showing up consistently"
- Inconsistent publishing — You start strong, then life happens. Your content schedule falls apart and you disappear from search results.
- SEO is expensive — Proper keyword research and SEO optimization costs money, so most businesses skip this phase and just guess.
- Search vs content — Your content doesn't match what people type into Google or ask AI tools.
- Momentum building — SEO takes time. You need months of consistent work to see results, and that's hard to maintain alone.

How to use Manus in your small business.

Manus is not a chatbot. It's an autonomous AI agent that can research, write, build, and deploy. This guide explains what that means and how to use it for tasks that would take you hours.

Open Manus Build Your Knowledge Base First

What makes Manus different.

ChatGPT and Claude are conversational. You ask a question, they answer. You give them a task, they complete it in one response. That's powerful, but it has limits.

Manus is an agent. It can take a complex goal, break it into steps, execute each step, use tools like web browsers and code editors, and deliver a finished result. It works autonomously, checking in when it needs guidance and completing the rest on its own.

For small business owners, this means you can hand Manus a task that would take you half a day and come back to a finished deliverable.

"Research the top 10 competitors in my market, summarize their content strategy, and identify the three biggest content gaps I can fill."

That's a task that takes a human 4-6 hours. Manus completes it in 20-30 minutes.

Best Use Cases

What Manus is best at for small businesses.

🔬

Competitive Research

Give Manus your competitors' URLs and ask it to analyze their content strategy, keyword rankings, and messaging. It browses, reads, and synthesizes the findings into a report.

📝

Long-Form Content Creation

Ask Manus to research a topic and write a comprehensive blog post or guide. It researches current information, structures the content, and delivers a polished draft.

📊

Data Analysis and Reports

Upload a spreadsheet or data file and ask Manus to analyze it, find patterns, and create a summary report. No Excel skills required.

🌐

Website and Page Building

Manus can build complete web pages, landing pages, and even full websites from a brief. This is how Ready, Plan, Grow! uses Manus to build and maintain its own site.

📧

Email Sequence Writing

Give Manus your product, your audience, and your goal. Ask it to write a 5-email welcome sequence. It plans the arc and writes all five emails in one session.

🗓️

Content Calendar Planning

Ask Manus to build a 30-day content calendar based on your target keywords and audience. It researches topics, plans the mix, and delivers a ready-to-use calendar.

How to give Manus a good task.

1

Be specific about the goal

Don't say "research my competitors." Say "Research the top 5 competitors for [your service] in [your city]. For each one, summarize their main services, their pricing if visible, and their top 3 content topics."

2

Specify the output format

Tell Manus what you want it to deliver. "Give me a structured report." "Create a spreadsheet." "Write a blog post." "Build an HTML page." The clearer the output requirement, the better the result.

3

Provide your context

Share your Business Backpack or key context at the start of the task. "Here is my brand knowledge base. Use it to make sure everything you create is on-brand."

4

Let it work

Unlike ChatGPT, Manus works autonomously. Give it the task and let it run. Check in when it asks for guidance. The less you interrupt, the better the result.

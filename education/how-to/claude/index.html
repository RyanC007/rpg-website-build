How to use Claude in your small business.

Claude by Anthropic is the best AI tool for long-form content and maintaining a consistent brand voice. This guide shows you when to use it and how to get the most out of it.

Open Claude Build Your Knowledge Base First

Why Claude is different from ChatGPT.

Claude and ChatGPT are both large language models, but they have different strengths. Claude tends to produce more nuanced, natural-sounding writing. It's better at maintaining a consistent tone across long documents. It's also better at following complex instructions without losing track of earlier context.

For small businesses, this means Claude is the better choice when you need:

Long-form blog posts

1,000+ word articles that maintain a consistent voice from start to finish. Claude handles length better than most AI tools.

Brand voice consistency

Content that sounds like your brand across multiple pieces. Claude is better at internalizing and maintaining voice guidelines.

Document analysis

Uploading a document and asking Claude to summarize, extract key points, or answer questions about it. Excellent for processing research and reports.

Complex instructions

Tasks with multiple requirements and constraints. Claude follows detailed instructions more reliably than most alternatives.

Step-by-Step

How to use Claude for blog content.

1

Create a free account at claude.ai

The free version is capable for most tasks. Claude Pro ($20/month) gives you access to the most powerful model and higher usage limits. Start free.

2

Upload your Business Backpack

Claude can accept document uploads. Upload your knowledge base document at the start of each session. Tell Claude: "This is my brand knowledge base. Use it as context for everything you write for me today."

3

Give it a detailed brief

For blog posts, include: target keyword, target audience, word count, key points to cover, and tone. The more specific the brief, the less editing you'll need to do.

4

Ask for a draft, then refine

Get the full draft first. Then ask Claude to adjust specific sections. "Make the introduction more direct." "Add a specific example in section 2." "Shorten the conclusion."

Frequently asked questions

Use Claude for long-form content, brand voice work, and document analysis. Use ChatGPT for quick tasks, brainstorming, and conversational use. Many business owners use both for different tasks.

The best approach is to load your Business Backpack with your brand voice guidelines, then give Claude 3-5 examples of your best existing content. Tell it: "Match the voice and style of these examples." The more examples you provide, the better the output.

The standard Claude interface does not browse the internet. Its knowledge has a training cutoff date. For research tasks requiring current information, use Manus or provide the source material directly.

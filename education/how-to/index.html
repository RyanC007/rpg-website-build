Choose Your Tool

Three tools. Three different strengths.

You don't need to use all three. Pick the one that fits your workflow and get good at it first.

🤖

ChatGPT

Best for: Quick tasks, brainstorming, emails

The most widely used AI tool. Great for quick content tasks, brainstorming, customer emails, and social captions. The easiest starting point for most business owners.

Claude

Best for: Long-form content, brand voice

Anthropic's Claude excels at long-form content and nuanced writing. It's better at maintaining a consistent brand voice across longer pieces. Great for blog posts and guides.

Manus

Best for: Research, complex tasks, automation

Before You Start

The one thing that makes all AI tools work better.

Every AI tool guide we publish assumes you have a Business Backpack — a knowledge base that holds your brand guidelines, customer profiles, and key messages. Without it, AI tools produce generic output. With it, they produce content that sounds like you.

If you don't have one yet, start there. It's the foundation that makes everything else work.

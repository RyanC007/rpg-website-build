What Is a Framework?

A framework is a repeatable system for solving a specific problem.

Instead of figuring out how to do something from scratch every time, a framework gives you a proven path. You follow the steps, fill in your specific details, and get a result that works. That's what we've built for small business marketing.

The Business Backpack

Your Knowledge Base Foundation

Your Business Backpack is the foundation that makes everything else work. It holds your brand guidelines, customer profiles, SEO strategy, competitive intelligence, and content library. All in one place. All yours.

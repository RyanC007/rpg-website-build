Marcela Shine

Operations and Marketing Expert

Marcela has spent 30+ years helping businesses build the operational and marketing foundations that allow them to grow without chaos. She co-founded Ready, Plan, Grow! because she kept seeing small business owners with great products and services who were invisible to their customers.

M

Her story.

Marcela started her career in operations and quickly realized that the best-run businesses were the ones where marketing and operations worked together, not in silos. She spent decades building and running marketing programs for companies across retail, professional services, and consumer brands.

When AI tools started changing the marketing landscape, Marcela saw an opportunity. Not to replace human strategy, but to amplify it. The businesses that would win weren't the ones with the biggest budgets. They were the ones with the clearest strategy and the most consistent execution.

That insight became Ready, Plan, Grow!

"Small businesses don't need more tools. They need a clear plan and someone to help them stick to it. That's what we built."
Marcela Shine, Co-Founder

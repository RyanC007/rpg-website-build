How It Works

Simple. Transparent. No hoops to jump through.

1

Apply to the program

Fill out a short application so we can understand your audience and how we can best support your referrals. Approval takes 1-2 business days.

2

Get your referral link

Once approved, you get a unique referral link and a partner dashboard where you can track clicks, signups, and earnings in real time.

3

Refer your clients

Share your link in your content, emails, or directly with clients who need content marketing or community support. We handle the rest.

4

Earn recurring commissions

Earn a commission every month for as long as your referred client stays with us. No caps. No expiration. Paid monthly via Stripe.

Featured Case Study

Elite Design Group

The Challenge

Elite Design Group is a boutique interior design firm with a strong portfolio and loyal clients. But they were getting almost all of their business through referrals. They had no consistent online presence, no SEO strategy, and no content marketing. When referrals slowed down, so did the pipeline.

They needed to build a marketing system that would bring in inbound leads without requiring them to become marketers.

What We Did

✓

Built their Business Backpack with brand guidelines, customer profiles, and SEO strategy

✓

Identified 6 high-value keyword clusters their ideal clients were searching for

✓

Built a 60-day content calendar and started publishing weekly blog posts

✓

Optimized their Google Business Profile and local SEO signals

The Results

Page 1

Ranking for target keywords within 4 months

3x

Increase in organic website traffic in 6 months

Inbound

First inbound leads from organic search within 90 days

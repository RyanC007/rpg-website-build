What We Check

Your audit covers every signal that matters.

Search engines and AI tools look at dozens of signals when deciding whether to recommend your business. Here's what we analyze.

🎯

Website Clarity

Can a visitor understand what you do and who you serve in under 10 seconds? We check your headline, subheadline, and above-the-fold messaging.

🗣️

Brand Voice Consistency

Does your website sound like one person, or does it feel like three different companies wrote it? Consistency builds trust. We check for it.

🔍

SEO Signals

Are your page titles, meta descriptions, and headings telling search engines what you do? We check the technical signals that affect your rankings.

🤖

AI Readability

When someone asks ChatGPT or Google's AI about businesses like yours, can it find and recommend you? We check how well your content is structured for AI tools.

📞

Call to Action Strength

Are your CTAs clear, specific, and placed where visitors are ready to act? Weak CTAs are one of the biggest conversion killers on small business websites.

📱

Mobile Experience

Over 60% of searches happen on mobile. We check whether your site works as well on a phone as it does on a desktop.

About Us

Built by Founders,
For Founders

One's a no-fluff operator who builds systems and tells it like it is. The other's an AI-obsessed founder who makes AI and SEO actually make sense.

Marcela and Ryan teamed up to create Ready, Plan, Grow! because they saw too many small business owners wasting time, overwhelmed by bad advice, confusing tools, and solutions that were over-priced for their small business. They see AI as the solution for most of these challenges.

Our Story

Ready, Plan, Grow! is a small business growth platform built for founders and small business owners who are great at running their business but don't have the time, support, or expertise to market it consistently or make confident decisions from their numbers. Without those two things, great businesses stall. We make sure yours doesn't.

We built Ready, Plan, Grow! (RPG) because we saw too many small business owners wasting money on overpriced agencies, drowning in confusing tools, and getting advice from people who had never actually built anything. Between us, we have over 30 years of real operating experience. Ryan in AI strategy, SEO, and ecommerce. Marcela in operations, marketing, and coaching over 15,000 entrepreneurs. We teach what we have done, not theory.

Everything starts with a Knowledge Base powered by the RPG Framework. Think of it as a backpack for your business. Everything about your brand voice, your ideal customer, and what makes you different, packed in one place and ready to go. We build it for you, or teach you to build it yourself. Either way, you own it completely and can take it anywhere. Any AI tool, any agency, any new hire. No vendor lock.

From there, you have two paths and a community behind both of them.

Done For You. Starting at $250/month, we plan, create, and publish optimized content every week so your business gets found on Google and recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT. Most clients see measurable search visibility improvements within 60 days.

Do It Yourself. For $50/month, you get our full framework library, business education, and tools, including Know Your Numbers, financial projections, and content strategy. Everything you need to stop guessing and start executing.

Community. Whether you choose Done For You or Do It Yourself, you are never doing this alone. You get a room full of founders at the same stage as you, free monthly workshops, and regular office hours with our team.

The free starting point? Join the community and attend office hours. No commitment. No credit card. Just show up.

Ready, Plan, Grow! Let's build it together.

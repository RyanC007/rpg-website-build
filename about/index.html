Why We Built This

The problem we kept seeing.

After decades working in marketing, operations, e-commerce, and AI, we kept seeing the same pattern. Small business owners who were great at what they did, but invisible online. Not because they weren't trying. Because the tools, the knowledge, and the time required to do marketing right were all built for companies with bigger budgets and bigger teams.

SEO agencies charge $3,000 a month. Marketing consultants charge by the hour. Content tools require technical expertise. And AI tools are powerful but generic without the right foundation.

We built Ready, Plan, Grow! to change that. A system that combines human strategy with AI execution, built specifically for small businesses, at a price that actually makes sense.

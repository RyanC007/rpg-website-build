Ryan Cunningham

AI Strategy and E-Commerce Expert

Ryan has spent his career building at the intersection of AI, e-commerce, and marketing technology. He co-founded Ready, Plan, Grow! to put enterprise-grade AI systems within reach of small businesses who can't afford a team of engineers.

R

His story.

Ryan started in e-commerce and quickly became obsessed with the systems that separate businesses that scale from businesses that stall. He spent years building and optimizing marketing technology stacks for e-commerce brands, learning what works and what's just expensive noise.

When AI tools started becoming genuinely useful for marketing, Ryan was already deep in the space. He saw that the businesses getting the most out of AI weren't the ones with the best prompts. They were the ones with the best foundations: clear brand guidelines, documented customer profiles, and a consistent content strategy.

That insight became the Business Backpack framework and the foundation of everything Ready, Plan, Grow! does.

"AI doesn't make bad strategy good. It makes good strategy faster. We build the strategy first. Then we let AI do the heavy lifting."
Ryan Cunningham, Co-Founder

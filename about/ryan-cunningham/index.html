AI Transformation

From Cancer Survivor to AI Pioneer

After surviving lung cancer, Ryan dove into AI and prompt engineering, founding 1der.ai to help small businesses cut through the hype and use AI tools that actually work.

Recognizing the transformative power of AI, Ryan, also known as "Rye The AI Guy" through his 1der.ai initiative, has spent the last three years immersed in AI research and prompt engineering.

His mission at 1der.ai is to demystify AI for small businesses, offering genuine advice and practical tools. This philosophy is born from over 25 years in internet marketing, coding, and SEO, and deepened by life experiences that taught him what truly matters.

What We Review

Every section that makes or breaks a pitch.

Investors make decisions in the first 3 minutes. We make sure your deck earns those 3 minutes and keeps them engaged.

📖

Your Story

Does your deck tell a compelling story? We check the narrative flow, the problem statement, and whether your solution is clearly positioned against the market.

💹

Financial Projections

Are your numbers believable and defensible? We review your revenue model, assumptions, and projections to make sure they hold up under investor scrutiny.

🗺️

Market Size

Is your TAM/SAM/SOM realistic and well-sourced? We validate your market size claims with independent research so you can defend them confidently.

👥

Team Slide

Does your team slide build confidence? We review how you're presenting your team's relevant experience and identify any gaps investors will ask about.

🎯

The Ask

Is your funding ask clear, specific, and tied to a concrete use of funds? Vague asks kill deals. We make sure yours is airtight.

🏆

Competitive Position

Does your competitive analysis show real differentiation? We check whether your positioning is credible and whether you've addressed the obvious objections.

What You Get Back

A complete review package, not just notes in the margins.

✓

Written review report with slide-by-slide feedback and priority fixes

✓

Financial projection review with commentary on assumptions and defensibility

✓

Independent market research to validate or challenge your TAM/SAM/SOM

✓

List of likely investor questions with suggested answers

Optional 60-minute debrief call to walk through the report together
